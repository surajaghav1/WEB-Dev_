ABOUT

Hello there! I'm Jennifer Locklin, the heart and soul behind Jennifer Cooks. With my roots as a trained chef and my heart in home cooking, I see my culinary journey not just as a passion but as a deep, generational connection that flavors every dish I create.

I live in Belton, Texas, with my husband Quinton—my college sweetheart from Sam Houston State University—and not far from the lively streets of Austin. This charming town is where we've raised our three children, who have since embarked on their own adventures.
Jennifer Locklin and her husband, Quinton, standing together in their kitchen. Here on my blog, each recipe is a proven favorite, tested and adored by us and our circle of friends and family. But these recipes are more than just instructions; they are your gateway to creating joyous, delicious memories.

My culinary journey began at The Art Institute of Houston and meandered through various high-energy restaurant kitchens, bakeries, and a catering career, where I specialized in wedding and birthday cakes.

However, life’s whirlwind brought me back to the heart of my home. In 2009, I launched this blog to keep a diary of my kitchen adventures.

One of my fondest memories is making peach fried pies in my grandmother’s kitchen. Standing on a sturdy chair because I couldn’t reach the counter, I helped her roll dough and spoon in luscious peach filling. Together, we’d fry the pies to a golden crisp and I’d finish them with a sprinkle of cinnamon sugar.

Those moments, infused with love and laughter, taught me that cooking is more than crafting food—it's a way to say “I love you.”
