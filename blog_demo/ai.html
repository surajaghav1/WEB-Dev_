What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn. AI is revolutionizing industries, from healthcare to automotive, by enabling machines to perform tasks that traditionally required human intelligence, such as decision-making, language translation, and even complex problem-solving.

Applications of AI

  • Healthcare: AI helps doctors diagnose diseases.
  • Finance: AI algorithms predict market trends.
  • Automotive: Self-driving cars use AI to navigate safely.
